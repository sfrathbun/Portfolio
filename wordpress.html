Search Engine Optimization

Are you on the first page of Google?

Here are 5 SEO freebies to get you started!

  1. Choose a keyword that best describes what people are searching for to find your product or content.
  2. Add images that have the same file name and alt tag as the keyword you are using. This tells what the picture is about.
  3. Give the meta description a power sentence that uses that same keyword and make it under 156 characters.
  4. Create some killer content for your page that is easy to read, and be sure to use transition words and keyword synonyms.
  5. Publish articles that back-link to your website. Also, don't forget to link internally to related content on your site.

Getting on the first page of Google isn't easy, especially when you're competing with 3,720,000,000 other websites if your trying to sell a pair of jeans. When it comes to Search Engine Optimization, it takes a solid plan and a person that is devoted to selling your product just as much as you are.
