About Me

Currently serving as Director of Project Delivery, with 5+ years of experience as a Security Engineer, I focus on building and operating backend systems, enterprise platforms, and forward-deployed solutions within security and compliance-driven environments. My work spans software development, system reliability, and hands-on problem solving in complex, real-world scenarios.

Technology, software development, and project execution have always been core passions of mine. I am deeply motivated by solving complex problems and guiding initiatives from initial concept through successful delivery. I believe the strongest outcomes are achieved through collaboration—by working closely with diverse teams, sharing ideas openly, and leveraging collective strengths to accomplish goals greater than any one individual could achieve alone. I am equally passionate about helping build and grow organizations by continuously improving systems, processes, and tooling, and by thoughtfully raising the bar on quality, performance, and innovation. Seeing tangible progress and sustainable growth is what energizes me most about the work I do.

Outside of my professional life, I place a high value on time spent with my family—especially my wife and our three young boys. We enjoy being outdoors together, whether that’s hiking, fishing, exploring the mountains, mountain biking, BMX riding, or seeking out new experiences that bring a sense of adventure. I’m always eager to take on new hobbies and challenges, fully committing myself to learning and growth. I believe in making the most of the life we’ve been given, embracing both meaningful work and memorable experiences along the way.
