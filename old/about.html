Hi, my name is Sean Rathbun,

I'm close to receiving my Full-Stack Web Developer Certification though Bethel School of Technology. I'm a motivated web developer with a passion to build innovative apps in our growing tech world. My strengths are teaching, communication, and project management. Growing in leadership and continuous learning are my top priorities as it comes to bettering myself and those around me. Outside of work, I enjoy being with family and spending time outdoors.

As a boy, I always loved pulling things apart and putting them back together again. Figuring things out and coming up with solutions always intrigued me. I was constantly building with whatever I could get my hands on. I grew to love carpentry because of the complexity and skill it takes to design and make a drawing come to life. There is a level of perfection in cabinetry and trim carpentry which cannot be overlooked; and it's always inspired me to get better.

Creativity was also something that came naturally to me which is why I picked up guitar, bass, mandolin, and vocals. Crafting original songs is something that makes me come alive; another part of building that I have always loved. With this background, it is no surprise that WordPress web development and coding have become my ultimate career choice. I believe that web development takes a high level of perfection, creativity and a drive to always improve. Through web development and coding, I've found my heart settled in a place where I can be free to create, build, and grow in the never ending world of technology.
